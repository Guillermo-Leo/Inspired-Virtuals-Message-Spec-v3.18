Basketball

Event codes

Basketball is event code 26 (US), 27 (European), or 71 (NBA).

Additional event card attributes

In the event card the following attributes are added to the event element

  • MeetingName - name of venue (e.g. Edgeley Arena).
  • HomeTeam - name of the home team (e.g. New York).
  • AwayTeam - name of the away team (e.g. Houston).
  • Details – details of the event in a string which can be displayed (e.g. “Houston @ New York”).
  • AwayUsesAlternateKit – 0 if the away team will play in their standard kit, 1 if they play in their alternate kit.

Additional result attributes

Under normal behaviour, the event element provides an additional child-element known as Result that contains the follow attributes(s):

  • HomePoints- Total home points.
  • AwayPoints- Total away points.
  • HomePointsFirstQuarter- Total home points in first quarter.
  • AwayPointsFirstQuarter- Total away points in first quarter.
  • HomePointsSecondQuarter- Total home points in second quarter.
  • AwayPointsSecondQuarter- Total away points in second quarter.
  • HomePointsThirdQuarter- Total home points in third quarter.
  • AwayPointsThirdQuarter- Total away points in third quarter.
  • HomePointsFourthQuarter- Total home points in fourth quarter.
  • AwayPointsFourthQuarter- Total away points in fourth quarter.

Markets and bet codes

The following list includes the most common markets offered, but it may not cover every available option for the event. Where an outcome contains "x.x" or "y.y" this will be replaced by a number that varies depending on the teams that are playing. Due to the lower scoring nature of european basketball some markets will have different outcomes in european basketball compared to US and NBA basketball.
