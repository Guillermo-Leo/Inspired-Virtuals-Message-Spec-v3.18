American Football

Event codes

American Football is event code 25 (Head to Head), 32 (NFLA), or 69 (NFL)

Additional event card attributes

In the event card the following attributes are added to the event element

  • MeetingName - name of venue (e.g. Edgeley Arena).
  • HomeTeam - name of the home team (e.g. Bobcats).
  • AwayTeam - name of the away team (e.g. Comets).
  • Details – details of the event in a string which can be displayed (e.g. “Bobcats vs Comets”).
  • HomeUsesAlternateKit – 0 if the home team will play in their standard kit, 1 if they play in their alternate kit.
  • AwayUsesAlternateKit – 0 if the away team will play in their standard kit, 1 if they play in their alternate kit.

Additional result attributes

Under normal behaviour, the event element provides an additional "Result" child-element that contains the follow attributes:

  • Home - Score of the home team
  • Away - Score of the away team

Markets and bet codes

The following list includes the most common markets offered, but it may not cover every available option for the event. Where an outcome contains "x.x" or "y.y" this will be replaced by a number that varies depending on the teams that are playing.

Spread

Betcode: 192

Outcomes:

  • "Home +x.x"
  • "Away -x.x"

Total Points Over/Under

Betcode: 193

Outcomes:

  • "Under x.x"
  • "Over x.x"

Money Line

Betcode: 194

Outcomes:

  • "Home"
  • "Away"

Winning Margin

Betcode: 195

Outcomes:

  • "Home 1-6"
  • "Home 7-12"
  • "Home 13-18"
  • "Home 19-24"
  • "Home 25-30"
  • "Home 31+"
  • "Away 1-6"
  • "Away 7-12"
  • "Away 13-18"
  • "Away 19-24"
  • "Away 25-30"
  • "Away 31+"

Total Points Groups

Betcode: 196

Outcomes:

  • "25 And Under"
  • "26-30"
  • "31-35"
  • "36-40"
  • "41-45"
  • "46-50"
  • "51 And Over"

Spread and Total Points Over/Under

Betcode: 197

Outcomes:

  • "Home + x.x and Under y.y"
  • "Home + x.x and Over y.y"
  • "Away - x.x and Under y.y"
  • "Away - x.x and Over y.y"

Money Line and Total Points Over/Under

Betcode: 198

Outcomes:

  • "Home and Under x.x "
  • "Home and Over x.x "
  • "Away and Under x.x "
  • "Away and Over x.x"
