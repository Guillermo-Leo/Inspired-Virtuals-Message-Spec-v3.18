Ice Hockey

Event codes

Ice Hockey is event code 72 (9 Clips) or 73 (12 Clips).

Additional event card attributes

In the event card the following attributes are added to the event element:

  • MeetingName - name of venue (e.g. "NHL").
  • HomeTeam - name of the home team (e.g. "Chicago Blackhawks").
  • AwayTeam - name of the away team (e.g. "New Jersey Devils").
  • HomeTeamShort – abbreviated name of the home team (e.g. "CHI").
  • AwayTeamShort – abbreviated name of the away team (e.g. "NJD").
  • Details – details of the event in a string which can be displayed, Home vs Away (e.g. "Chicago vs New Jersey").
  • HomeUsesAlternateKit – 0 if the home team will play in their standard kit, 1 if they play in their alternate kit.
  • AwayUsesAlternateKit – 0 if the away team will play in their standard kit, 1 if they play in their alternate kit.

Additional result attributes

Under normal behaviour, the event element provides an additional "Result" child-element that contains the follow attributes:

  • Home - score of the home team.
  • Away - score of the away team.
  • PeriodExacta - which team was winning at the end of each period (e.g. "AAA", represents away team winning at the end of each period). "H" represents Home, "A" represents Away, "T" represents Tie.
  • FirstGoalScorer - team and playing position of first goal scorer (e.g. "Away Right Wing").
